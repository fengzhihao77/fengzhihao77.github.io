About

I build physics-grounded ML workflows and simulations spanning across DFT/TDDFT, atomistic MD, and coarse-graining MD, especially for polymer semiconductors and sustainable polymers. I developed a physics-informed ML surrogate for the polymer integral equation theory from 370+ GB CGMD data (~400 systems; SANS-validated; >90% benchmark wins) and built pyForceTune to optimize force fields against DFT reference data (46% lower binding-energy error; <0.1 kcal/mol dihedral error) for multiscale modeling to guide thin-film processing decisions.
